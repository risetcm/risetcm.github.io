About

Treatments — Acupuncture

Fine needles, precise effect.

Acupuncture uses hair-thin needles at specific points to move qi, ease tension, and prompt the body's own regulatory systems into balance. It's the foundation of most treatment plans at Rise.

What to Expect

What a session looks like

A conversation about your health history before any needling begins
Thin, sterile, single-use needles placed at targeted points
Most people feel little more than a light pinch, then deep relaxation
Sessions run 45–60 minutes, often finishing with quiet rest time
Effects build over a series of visits, not just one
Frequency tapers as your symptoms improve

Best For

Conditions this can help

Point selection is never generic — it's based on your presenting pattern, pulse, and tongue diagnosis, and adjusted at every visit as your body responds. Acupuncture is often paired with herbal medicine or moxibustion for a fuller effect.
