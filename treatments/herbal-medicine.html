Treatments — Herbal Medicine

What to Expect

What a session looks like

Formulas drawn from a pharmacopoeia refined over centuries
Custom-adjusted to your pattern, not a fixed over-the-counter blend
Available as raw herbs, granules, or pills depending on your routine
Reviewed and adjusted at every follow-up visit
Sourced from tested, quality-controlled suppliers
Used alongside, not instead of, any medications you're prescribed

Best For

Conditions this can help
